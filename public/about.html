About

At Blue Fig Labs, we want to make the world's workplaces healthier by building tools for wellbeing.

Our first app is Gratitude.

We ask a simple question: "What are you grateful for today?"

Research shows that recording 3 gratitudes a day for 3 weeks (21 days) can make you happier and healthier (producing more sleep, more energy, positive thoughts and an improved outlook on life - all contributing to increased wellbeing).

Gratitude is the pilot app in a comprehensive wellbeing package that covers health, happiness, and healing.

Health: According to leading gratitude and happiness researchers, keeping a gratitude journal can result in better sleep and more energy, increasing overall well-being and health.

Happiness: People who regularly practice grateful thinking can increase their set point of happiness by as much as 25 percent.

Healing: Gratitude art is inspired by mandala-like grids created by a visionary artist and researcher as instruments of healing.
